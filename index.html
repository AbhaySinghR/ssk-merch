THE LEGACY

Heritage, in three movements.

Every collection we release is rooted in the same institution that shaped us — its history, its purpose, and the community it continues to build long after we walked out of its gates.

I

School History

Founded in 1961, Sainik School Kapurthala has shaped generations of students who went on to serve, build, lead, and create — each carrying a part of these grounds with them.

II

Our Mission

To carry forward the discipline, friendships, and pride found within these walls into the everyday lives of every Saikapian, one piece of merchandise at a time.

III

Alumni Community

A growing community spanning professions, countries, and decades — united not by one path, but by the same school, the same memories, and the same pride.

WHY THIS MERCH EXISTS

More Than Merchandise

This is not just apparel. It is a reminder of morning assemblies, dorm life, lifelong friendships, house spirit, school pride, and the journey that shaped every Saikapian in a different way.

This platform celebrates all of us — the ones serving, the ones building, the ones leading, and the ones carrying the name of Sainik School Kapurthala across the world.
